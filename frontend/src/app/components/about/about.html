Our Mission

Many students face uncertainty when selecting a university major due to a lack of self-awareness and access to proper academic advising. Our mission is to offer a smart, AI-powered solution that provides personalized and informed guidance based on each student's academic profile and interests.

What We Do

  • Assess students using interactive quizzes and academic input forms.
  • Analyze grades, achievements, and personal projects.
  • Use a hybrid AI approach combining rule-based logic and ML models like Ollama and Mistral.
  • Present tailored major recommendations with explanations, confidence scores, and suggestions.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: Angular
  • Backend: FastAPI
  • AI Engine: Rule-based + Machine Learning (Ollama / Mistral / LLaMA 3)
  • Database: PostgreSQL or MySQL
  • Deployment: Docker + Docker Compose

Why It Matters

Selecting the right major is a crucial decision. Our tool empowers students with AI-based insights to help them make confident, informed choices that align with their abilities and aspirations.

Thank you for using our system. We hope it serves as a reliable guide in your academic journey!
